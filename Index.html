About Air Pollution

Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the air we breathe. It is a major environmental issue affecting people worldwide.

Causes of Air Pollution

  • Vehicle Emissions
  • Industrial Activities
  • Deforestation
  • Agricultural Practices
  • Household Combustion

Effects of Air Pollution

Air pollution can lead to various health problems such as respiratory diseases, cardiovascular issues, and even premature death. It also contributes to environmental degradation and climate change.

Solutions to Air Pollution

  • Use of Clean Energy Sources
  • Improving Public Transportation
  • Implementing Stricter Regulations on Industries
  • Promoting Tree Plantation
  • Reducing Waste Generation
